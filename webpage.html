Who We Are

About InAmigos Foundation

2020 Founded

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a Central Government-licensed Section 8 non-profit organization headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh.

Our foundation believes that change begins with compassion and collective action. We work across multiple states — Chhattisgarh, Delhi, Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh, and Madhya Pradesh — addressing hunger, illiteracy, gender inequality, environmental degradation, and unemployment.

Our Vision

To create a world where every underprivileged person has the opportunity to grow with dignity, respect, and equal access to education, nutrition, and economic opportunity — regardless of background or circumstance.

Our Mission

To unite driven individuals and organizations in creating lasting, positive change in society through transparent, community-driven programs in education, nutrition, women's empowerment, environmental sustainability, and skill development.

Our Values

Compassion · Transparency · Collective Action · Sustainability · Inclusion · Accountability. We hold ourselves to the highest standards so every rupee raised creates maximum, measurable impact.

What We Do

Our Flagship Projects

Six transformative initiatives — each designed to break a different barrier and build a more equitable India.

Nutrition & Welfare

Project SEVA

Providing nutritious meals and essential clothing to underprivileged communities, ensuring dignity and warmth for those who need it most. Every distribution drive spreads hope and raises awareness about food security.

50,000+ Meals & Clothing Items Distributed
Education

Project BACHPANSHALA

Breaking the cycle of poverty through quality education — arranging learning sessions, providing school supplies, and introducing digital literacy to underprivileged children in rural areas.

Hundreds of Children Reached Across Rural India
Women Empowerment

Project UDAAN

Uplifting women through skill development workshops, digital awareness campaigns, and entrepreneurship training — enabling financial independence and leadership in underserved communities.

900+ Girls Empowered Through Digital Campaigns
Environment

Project PRAKRITI

Protecting the planet through tree-planting drives, community clean-up campaigns, and sustainability workshops — instilling environmental responsibility in local communities for future generations.

Tree Plantation Drives Across Multiple States
Animal Welfare

Project JEEV

Feeding and protecting stray animals, promoting compassion towards all living beings, and advocating for animal welfare in communities where they are most vulnerable.

50+ Animals Fed & Cared for Daily
Skill Development

Project VIKAS

Bridging the employment gap by providing professional internship training in data operations, finance, digital marketing, content writing, research, and social work to youth across India.

30,000+ Interns Trained Over 4 Years
